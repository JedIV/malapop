What's the Buzz?

MALAPOP is Szechuan peppercorn popcorn that hits different. We took the iconic málà tingle from the streets of Chengdu and crashed it into America's favorite snack.

Every kernel is kissed with real Szechuan peppercorn, chili, and a whisper of citrus that lights up your tongue. It's not just spicy — it's electric.

Made in small batches. Based in New York City. Zero chill.
